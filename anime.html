HUNTER MEDE'S ANIME OBSESSION

For 3 years straight, I would go to New York's Comic Con and LA's Anime Expo. Obviously, last year was a no go due to Covid, but I'm hoping this year will go back to normal.

My dad and I took a guys' trip to Japan. We ate tons of my favorites: ramen, udon, and sushi. The highlight of the trip was when we went to the famous Akihabara Crossing in Tokyo... it was like the heavens opened! Akihabara has 10 floors filled with shops specializing in anime, manga (anime comic books), and video games!
